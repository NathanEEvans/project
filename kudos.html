Credits's & Kudos

We tend to forget and take for granted that we can get loads of software for ziltsj, nakko, nada, um zonst, to help us with creating yet more awesome software. Well, we should not. It's quite remarkable that there are people out there who, simply because they like to create stuff and are not afraid of sharing it, bring you free software. No strings attached. Awesome that is i say.

Here we would like to says thanks and point you to them. In no particular order. Random, not ranked. They are all very cool.

Dojo Toolkit

Because a poweful and feature rich Javascript Toolkit like Dojo is just around for us to use we have achieved great results on the GUI. No idea how that would ever have happened without Dojo. Pay them a visit and check out the incredible cool things they do.

Apache Tomcat

Since many years very hapily using the Apache Tomcat Servlet Container. This is after the servlet technology itself the most useful piece of software in millennia. That is together with all the other great software seeing daylight at The Apache Software Foundation
